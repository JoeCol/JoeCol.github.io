Short Biography
I am an early career researcher with a doctorate in computer science, with multiple publications and with a strong background working in industry. My research interests include AI, multi-agent systems, AI and law, and robotics. I look towards bringing multiple disciplines together with computer science, with an aim of realising developments into real world applications.

Currently working with Louise Dennis and Micheal Fisher, I am currently exploring how regulations and ethics can be captured and used to verify multi/agent systems such as autonomous cars and UAV's.
